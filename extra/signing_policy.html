Key Signing:

I am willing to sign keys at keysigning parties and conferences. Please, do not email me requesting that I sign a key if we do not already know each other.

Signature Requirements:

I am willing to sign the following types of keys:
  • Personal Keys
  • Group and Shared Keys
  • Corporate Keys
  • Pseudononymous Keys
My requirements per signature trust level are as follows:
  • 0x10: Generic certification
    • Two forms of valid picture identification. At least one must be government issued.

  • 0x11: Persona certification
    • Reasonable proof of identity, such as:
      • Ability to prove key pair control through decrypting of a message.
      • Identifying documents.
      • I have previous knowledge of establishment of your asserted identity

  • 0x12: Casual certification
    • Two forms of valid picture identification. At least one must be government issued.

  • 0x13: Positive certification
    • Two forms of valid government issued picture identification.
    • I must have had a personal or professional relationship with you for at least five (5) years.
